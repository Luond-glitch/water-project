How to Recycle Plastic Bottles

Step 1: Collect Bottles

Gather all your plastic bottles. Make sure to rinse them to remove any residue.

Step 2: Remove Caps and Labels

Take off the caps and labels, as they are often made from different materials.

Step 3: Flatten Bottles

Flatten the bottles to save space in your recycling bin.

Step 4: Sort the Bottles

Sort the bottles by type (e.g., PETE, HDPE) if your recycling program requires it.

Step 5: Take to Recycling Center

Drop off the sorted bottles at your local recycling center or place them in your curbside recycling bin.

Step 6: Educate Others

Share your knowledge about recycling with friends and family to promote awareness.
